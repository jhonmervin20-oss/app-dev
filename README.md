## Story Overview
The movie centers on two estranged sisters, Ellie and Beth. Ellie is a single mother living in a rundown apartment, while Beth is her younger sister who comes to visit. Everything begins to go wrong when the children discover a mysterious book hidden in the building, known as the Necronomicon or The Book of the Dead. When a recording of the book’s chant is played, it awakens a demonic force called the Deadites, which ends up possessing Ellie. From that point on, the story turns into a terrifying battle for survival inside the apartment, filled with intense horror, disturbing gore, and strong emotional tension.
